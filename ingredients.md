These are the ingredients for the delicious Gulab Jamun:-

1) FOR JAMUN:
milk powder(100 grams)
maida(60 grams)
baking powder(1/2 tsp)
ghee(2 tbsp)

2)FOR SUGAR SYRUP:-
water(2 cups)
sugar(2 cups)
cardamom(2)
kesar(1/4 tsp)
lemon juice(1 tsp)
rose water(1 tsp)
